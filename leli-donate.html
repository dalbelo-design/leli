Why It Matters

Better policy comes from better policy design.

Contra Costa County is investing deeply in its strategic plan to reduce homelessness. That plan gets stronger when the people who've navigated the system help steer it — and too often, that perspective enters the room after key decisions are already made.

LELI changes the design table. We train people with lived experience of homelessness to co-author the work — analyzing community input, mapping where services connect and where they fall short, translating firsthand knowledge into the language of decision-making, and bringing it directly into the rooms where the plan is shaped, implemented, and evaluated.

When lived experience helps design the plan, the plan works better. The dollars do more. The systems do better.

Fiscal Sponsorship

About Realize Impact.

LELI operates under the charitable umbrella of Realize Impact — a registered 501(c)(3) that makes your gift fully tax-deductible.

LELI is fiscally sponsored by Realize Impact, a 501(c)(3) public charity specializing in fiscal sponsorship of mission-driven projects. Realize Impact provides the charitable umbrella, financial oversight, grants administration, and compliance infrastructure that lets LELI focus entirely on Fellows and communities.

Fiscal sponsorship means your gift is made to Realize Impact on behalf of LELI. It is fully tax-deductible, subject to all 501(c)(3) rules, and directed entirely to LELI's programming — Fellow compensation, training, and the development of the Civic Signals Network.

All donations, whether made online, by check, or by wire transfer, are processed through Realize Impact. When you give, please notify us through our contact form so we can ensure your gift is properly allocated.

LELI is a project of Realize Impact, a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN available upon request). All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. No goods or services were provided in exchange for your contribution.
